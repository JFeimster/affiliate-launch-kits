## Prompt

```text
Create a professional landing page for a Moonshine Capital partner focused on accountant referral relationships.

Goal: help accountants understand how the partner can be a useful next-step resource when business clients need to explore capital options for operations, growth, equipment, cash flow, or time-sensitive business needs.

Sections:
1. Hero
2. Who this helps
3. Common client situations
4. How the partner supports the relationship
5. Simple process
6. FAQ
7. Final CTA
8. Responsible-use footer

Design direction:
- clean fintech style
- professional and restrained
- easy to skim
- strong trust signals
- mobile friendly

Tone: respectful, practical, and relationship-first.

Do not position accountants as sales channels. Position the page as a helpful resource for their clients when appropriate.
```
