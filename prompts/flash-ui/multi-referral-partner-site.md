# Multi Referral Partner Site Prompt

Use this prompt to create a landing page for multiple referral partner audiences.

## Asset Status

- Platform: Flash-UI

## Prompt

```text
Create a professional landing page for Moonshine Capital referral partner relationships.

Goal: help professionals understand how they can be a useful resource for business owners in their network who need to explore possible capital paths.

Target audiences:
- Bank managers
- Accountants
- Attorneys
- Equipment dealers
- Business consultants
- Local connectors

Sections:
1. Hero
2. Who this is for
3. Why referral partners matter
4. Common business situations
5. How the process works
6. Audience-specific cards
7. FAQ
8. Final CTA
9. Responsible-use footer

Design direction:
- clean fintech layout
- high-trust cards
- strong audience segmentation
- mobile friendly
- clear CTA buttons

Tone: professional, respectful, and relationship-first.

Do not frame referral partners as lead mules. Frame them as trusted resources who can help their existing network access a responsible next step.
```
