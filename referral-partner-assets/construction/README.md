# Construction Referral Assets

Canonical source assets for contractors, subcontractors, construction suppliers, equipment rental contacts, trade networks, and project-based business connectors.

## Audience

Referral sources connected to construction businesses that need capital for materials, payroll, equipment, job mobilization, rentals, repairs, receivables, progress payments, or working capital.

## Core Pain Points

- Jobs can be ready before cash is available.
- Contractors often need materials, labor, rentals, fuel, permits, or equipment before customers pay.
- Progress payments, draws, retainage, and unpaid invoices can choke otherwise healthy businesses.
- A funding review can help contractors keep projects moving when cash timing is the bottleneck.

## Partner Variant Strategy

Use these canonical assets as the source. Create a partner-specific version under `launch-kits/{partner-name}/` only when adding partner contact info, QR/application link, local market notes, partner-specific lanes, or launch-specific positioning.
