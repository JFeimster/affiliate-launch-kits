# Equipment Dealer Referral Assets

Canonical source assets for equipment dealers, repair shops, commercial truck sellers, machinery vendors, suppliers, and business-use equipment contacts.

## Audience

Referral sources whose customers need trucks, trailers, machinery, yellow iron, work vehicles, shop equipment, tools, repairs, or revenue-producing assets.

## Core Pain Points

- Customers want to buy, repair, or upgrade equipment but funding blocks the sale.
- Paying cash can drain the business and create the next cash-flow problem.
- Bank delays can kill time-sensitive equipment sales or repairs.
- Dealers and vendors can preserve sales by pointing customers to a funding review.

## Canonical Assets

Recommended source files:

- `one-pager.md`
- `printable-handout.md`
- `outreach-script.md`
- `follow-up-script.md`
- `landing-page-copy.md`

## Partner Variant Strategy

Use these canonical assets as the source. Create a partner-specific version under `launch-kits/{partner-name}/` only when adding partner contact info, QR/application link, local market notes, partner-specific lanes, or launch-specific positioning.
