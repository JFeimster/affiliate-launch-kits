## Positioning

Use this script to introduce `[Partner Name]` as a responsible next-step funding resource for business clients who do not currently fit traditional bank criteria.

The angle is not anti-bank.

The angle is:

> When the bank has to say “not right now,” the banker can still protect the relationship by giving the client a practical next step.

---

## Walk-In / In-Person Script

Hi, I’m `[Partner Name]`. I work with Moonshine Capital as a commercial funding partner.

I’m not here to compete with the bank. I’m here to be a resource for the business clients you cannot help right now — the ones who may be solid operators but do not fit the bank’s box today because of timing, collateral, credit profile, documentation, or underwriting limits.

When that happens, I can help them explore possible funding options through one short application.

The goal is not to promise anything wild. The goal is to give the client a responsible next step instead of sending them into random online lending chaos.

Would it be helpful if I left you a simple referral sheet so you know who may be a good fit?
