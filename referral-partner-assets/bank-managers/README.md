# Bank Manager Referral Assets

Canonical source assets for bank managers, branch managers, business bankers, commercial bankers, and credit union business contacts.

## Audience

Banking professionals who occasionally have business clients they cannot fund today because of underwriting criteria, collateral, documentation, credit profile, timing, or bank policy.

## Core Pain Points

- Declined or delayed business clients still need a next step.
- Bankers want to preserve the relationship without changing underwriting standards.
- Clients may need speed, working capital, equipment funding, receivables support, or bridge capital.
- A responsible referral can help clients avoid random online lending chaos.

## Canonical Assets

Recommended source files:

- `one-pager.md`
- `printable-handout.md`
- `outreach-script.md`
- `follow-up-script.md`
- `landing-page-copy.md`

## Partner Variant Strategy

Use these canonical assets as the source. Create a partner-specific version under `launch-kits/{partner-name}/` only when adding partner contact info, QR/application link, local market notes, partner-specific lanes, or launch-specific positioning.
