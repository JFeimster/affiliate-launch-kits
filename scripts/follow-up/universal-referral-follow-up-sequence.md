# Universal Referral Follow-Up Sequence

**Asset Type:** Canonical script  
**Audience:** Referral partners, professional contacts, business connectors  
**Partner-Specific:** No  

---

## Purpose

Use this sequence to follow up with referral sources after an intro, networking conversation, email, or referral partner call.

The goal is to stay useful without sounding like a desperate LinkedIn goblin with a quota problem.

---
