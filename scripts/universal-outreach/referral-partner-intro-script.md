# Universal Referral Partner Intro Script

**Asset Type:** Canonical script  
**Audience:** Referral partners, local connectors, professional networks, business service providers  
**Partner-Specific:** No  

---

## Positioning

Use this script when introducing Moonshine Capital as a funding review resource for people who already know business owners with capital problems.

The angle:

> Your network already has business owners dealing with cash-flow pressure. You do not need to become a lender. You can become the trusted connector who gives them a practical next step.

---

## Short In-Person Script

I work with Moonshine Capital helping business owners explore funding options when capital becomes the bottleneck.

That could be working capital, equipment, payroll, receivables, repairs, startup costs, bank delays, or growth funding.

I’m not asking you to become a lender or change what you do. I’m simply a resource you can point business owners to when they need a practical next step.

If someone in your network is stuck because money timing is ugly, send them my way and I’ll help them start a funding review.
