# Business Owner Intake Call Script

**Asset Type:** Canonical script  
**Audience:** Business owners / funding applicants  
**Partner-Specific:** No  

---

## Purpose

Use this script for a first conversation with a business owner who needs funding options reviewed.

The goal is to quickly understand the business, the funding need, the urgency, and whether the person should complete the application.

---

## Opening

Thanks for taking the time to connect.

I’ll keep this simple. My goal is to understand what you need capital for, what the business looks like, and whether it makes sense for you to complete the funding review application.

I cannot guarantee approval, amounts, terms, cost, or timing, but the application can help identify possible paths based on your business profile.

---

## Core Questions

1. What does the business do?
2. How long has the business been operating?
3. How much funding are you looking for?
4. What will the funds be used for?
5. How urgent is the need?
6. What is your average monthly revenue?
7. Do you use a business bank account or personal bank account for deposits?
8. Are you currently waiting on invoices, customer payments, contracts, or receivables?
9. Do you need equipment, repairs, payroll, inventory, materials, working capital, startup capital, or growth funding?
10. Have you already applied anywhere else?
11. Were you declined by a bank or lender recently?
12. Are there any current advances, loans, or funding balances already in place?
13. Are you willing to complete a short application and provide documents if needed?

---

## Funding Use Case Match

Listen for these signals:

| Signal | Possible Direction |
|---|---|
| Urgent small need | Fast Working Capital |
| Equipment / truck / machinery | Equipment & Truck Financing |
| Waiting on invoices | Invoice & Receivables Funding |
| Larger growth need | Larger Funding Review |
| New business / startup | Startup Funding Path |
| Recurring cash-flow gaps | Business Line Access |
| Bank decline | Alternative funding review |
