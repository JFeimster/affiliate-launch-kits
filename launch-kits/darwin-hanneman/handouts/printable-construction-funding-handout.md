## Common Pain Points

### The Job Is Won. The Cash Is Not Ready.

Materials, labor, permits, fuel, equipment rental, vendors, and subs need payment before the customer, GC, or draw schedule catches up.

### The Contractor Is Waiting to Get Paid.

The work may be done, but the money is trapped in someone else’s payment cycle. A progress payment, invoice, or retainage holdback can choke a good business.

### Equipment, Repairs, or Rentals Are Holding Up the Work.

A skid steer, dump truck, trailer, lift, loader, excavator, or tool package can be the difference between taking the job and watching it die.
