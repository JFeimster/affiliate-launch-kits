## Purpose

These scripts help Darwin start real conversations with referral partners and business owners without sounding like a spam cannon in a cheap blazer.

The positioning is simple:

**Darwin helps business owners explore practical funding options through Moonshine Capital when banks are slow, traditional financing does not fit, or cash flow becomes the bottleneck.**

The goal is not to pressure people.

The goal is to create trust, identify real funding needs, and move qualified prospects toward a simple next step.

---

## Core Positioning Line

Use this as the backbone for most conversations:

> “I help business owners explore practical funding options when traditional financing is delayed, unavailable, or not the right fit. One short application helps identify possible funding paths based on the business profile.”

---

## Compliance Guardrails

Avoid saying or implying:

- “Everyone qualifies.”
- “Approval is guaranteed.”
- “Funding is guaranteed.”
- “You’ll get funded today.”
- “Credit does not matter.”
- “We approve what banks deny.”
- “Any business can get any amount.”
- “This is free money.”
- “This will not affect your business.”
- “The bank failed you.”

Use safer language:

- “Explore funding options.”
- “May be available.”
- “Based on business profile.”
- “Provider criteria apply.”
- “Approval, funding amount, terms, and timing are not guaranteed.”
- “The application helps identify possible funding paths.”

# 1. Bank Manager Walk-In Script

## Situation

Darwin walks into a local bank branch or meets a banker in person.

## Script

Hi, I’m Darwin Hanneman. I work with Moonshine Capital as a commercial funding partner.

I’m not here to compete with the bank. I’m looking to be a resource for the business clients you can’t help right now — the ones who may be solid operators but don’t fit the bank’s box today because of timing, collateral, credit profile, documentation, or underwriting limits.

When that happens, I can give them a responsible next step. One short application helps us explore possible funding paths based on their business profile.

If nothing makes sense, we’ll be honest. If there may be an option, we’ll walk them through next steps without making wild promises.

Would it be helpful if I left you a simple one-page referral sheet so you know who may be a good fit?

## Soft CTA

> “What kind of business clients do you most often have to turn away or delay?”

## Leave-Behind Line

> “If you ever have a business owner who needs a next step after the bank says not right now, send them my way.”
