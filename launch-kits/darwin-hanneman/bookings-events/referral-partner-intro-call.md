# Darwin Referral Partner Intro Call

**Asset Type:** Booking / intro call copy  
**Partner:** Darwin Hanneman  
**Brand:** Moonshine Capital  
**Recommended Duration:** 15–20 minutes  
**Audience:** Bank managers, accountants, attorneys, equipment dealers, trucking contacts, construction connectors, and local business referral sources

---

## Booking Service Name

Funding Referral Partner Intro Call

## Purpose

Use this call to explain how Darwin can help referral sources give business owners a practical next step when funding is the bottleneck.

This is not a hard pitch. It is a relationship-first intro.

---

## Public Booking Description

A short intro call for professionals, business connectors, and referral sources who know business owners that may need funding options.

This call is a fit for bank managers, accountants, attorneys, equipment dealers, trucking contacts, construction connectors, and local business professionals who want a trusted resource for clients or contacts dealing with working capital, equipment, payroll, receivables, repairs, bank delays, or growth funding needs.

---

## Who This Is For

This intro call is for people who know business owners dealing with:

- bank declines or slow approvals
- urgent working capital needs
- equipment, truck, trailer, or machinery purchases
- repairs or cash-flow gaps
- payroll, materials, inventory, fuel, or vendor pressure
- unpaid invoices or receivables
- startup, expansion, or growth funding needs

---

## What They’ll Learn

During the call, the referral source will learn:

- who is a good referral
- what funding situations may be reviewed
- how the referral process works
- how the application link or QR code can be shared
- how Darwin handles the funding conversation
- what not to promise or guarantee
- how referral relationships may create additional revenue opportunities

---

## Call Agenda

1. Quick intro and relationship context
2. What types of business owners they know
3. Common funding pain points in their network
4. How Darwin / Moonshine Capital can help review possible options
5. Referral process and next step
6. Share relevant one-pager or handout
7. Confirm follow-up action

---

## Pre-Call Questions

Use these as optional booking form questions:

1. What type of business owners do you usually work with or know?
2. Which funding situations do you see most often?
   - bank decline
   - working capital
   - equipment / truck financing
   - payroll or materials
   - unpaid invoices
   - startup funding
   - growth or expansion
   - other
3. Do you currently have someone in mind who may need funding options?
4. Would you prefer a referral link, QR code, handout, or all of the above?
